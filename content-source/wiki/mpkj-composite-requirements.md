# MPKj composite requirements (Kajang)

**Status:** emerging
**Last updated:** 2026-06-21
**Sources:** `RAW/Forms/Majlis Perbandaran Kajang/Panduan permohonan Lesen perniagaan MPKJ.pdf`

## Summary

Majlis Perbandaran Kajang (MPKj) licenses business premises under its Jabatan Pelesenan & Penjaja (Licensing and Hawker Department) across three licence classes — trade, business, and industrial. The general document set asks for most items in four copies, carries a stated process fee of RM50 for business and RM100 for industrial, and requires a BOMBA fire-safety support letter up front. MPKj's activity table is built around the Hulu Langat district: liquor is licensed by the Hulu Langat District/Land Office and the swiftlet trade needs Hulu Langat PERHILITAN support, which matters when scoping any add-on licences.

## Body

### General documents

For the trade/business/industrial licence the applicant supplies: one passport-sized photo; one IC or passport copy (for non-citizens); one company registration certificate (Form M&A and Form 49, or Form A, or Form B plus Form D); one tenancy agreement or proof of building ownership, stamped or endorsed by a Commissioner for Oaths; four copies of the CF (or temporary building permit, or land grant); four copies of the current, paid assessment tax bill; four copies of the business signboard visual (computer-produced); four floor plans; a fire-safety support letter from BOMBA; four business location plans; four front and four rear photos plus one side photo and two interior photos in colour; the process fee (RM50 for business, RM100 for industrial); and confirmation from the Valuation and Property Management Department where needed.

### Activity-specific additions

Food premises need a valid TY2 injection, a food-handling course certificate, and two photos of an installed grease trap. A beauty or health-care centre needs a health-inspection report. A factory needs a production flow chart, a JAS support letter where relevant, and a KDN letter for printing factories only. Used-goods (barang lusuh) trading in an industrial area needs a used-goods licence from the Kajang District Police Headquarters. Childcare, kindergarten and tuition centres need a JKM certificate (childcare), State Education Department approval (kindergarten/tuition), a building-use change approval from residential to commercial issued by the Development Planning Department, and a food-handling certificate plus TY2 where applicable. Liquor needs a licence from the Hulu Langat District/Land Office. Video/VCD needs a FINAS certificate and a KPDNKK support letter. A clinic or medical centre needs a copy of the practitioner's certificate from the Malaysian Medical Association. A council stall (gerai majlis) needs an offer letter from the MPKj Valuation Department and receipts for stall rent, utilities and deposit. The swiftlet (burung walit) trade needs a bird-nest course certificate, Veterinary Department support, and a support letter from PERHILITAN Hulu Langat.

### Team-logged nuances

The MPKj application form is bought and submitted over the counter, needs a real (wet) signature and the company stamp, and the BOMBA support letter is collected from the Hulu Langat branch. Liquor and swiftlet add-ons both route through Hulu Langat district offices rather than the council, so build that lead time into the scope.
