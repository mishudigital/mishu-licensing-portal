# MBSJ composite requirements (Subang Jaya)

**Status:** emerging
**Last updated:** 2026-06-21
**Sources:** `RAW/Forms/Majlis Bandaraya Subang Jaya/8a. SENARAI SEMAK UMUM (SM6B)(1).pdf`, `RAW/Forms/Majlis Bandaraya Subang Jaya/Checklist.docx`

## Summary

Majlis Bandaraya Subang Jaya (MBSJ, the council for the MPSJ area) issues its general business-and-advertisement licence against the SM6B / Biasa checklist of eleven items. The identifying MBSJ quirk is that every document copy must be crossed through and marked "Urusan MBSJ sahaja". Signboards follow the MPSJ Advertising Bylaw 2007, which sets the national language at 30% larger than any other language and fixes the position of the activity wording and the licence number on the board. The applicant self-prints an acknowledgement account at the start, which points to an online intake step.

## Body

### General documents (SM6B / Biasa checklist)

The eleven-item set is: the self-printed acknowledgement account (cetakan akaun penerimaan); one IC copy for citizens, or passport and Trade Visa details for non-citizens, with the copy crossed through and marked "Urusan MBSJ sahaja"; one passport-sized photo attached to the form; the SSM registration certificate; the SSM name-change documents where applicable; the CF or CCC; the stamped tenancy or sale-and-purchase agreement, or the land grant for private land; one location plan or a clearly marked Google Maps printout; one floor-plan sketch with the premises area noted; current premises photos (front, rear, interior, and side for a corner lot); and the signboard visual with measurements in square metres carrying DBP grammar approval, which is valid for one year only.

The SSM portion accepts several forms of proof: for a Sdn Bhd, Berhad or PLT, either Form 9/13, 24 and 49 with the MAA, or the Certificate of Incorporation with Particulars of Company and Particulars of Director, or the PLT registration with the List of Partners; for a sole proprietor, Form D (Kaedah 13) with the business and owner information.

### Signboard rules (MPSJ Advertising Bylaw 2007)

The national language must be 30% larger than any other language on the board. The premises activity is placed in the top-right corner of the advertisement, and the MPSJ reference number (the licence number) in the bottom-right corner. Language confirmation must be obtained from Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka via dbp.gov.my before submission.

### Team-logged nuance

MBSJ insists that document copies are crossed and marked "Urusan MBSJ sahaja" — the same convention noted in the cross-council composite article. A shared MISHU working checklist (the English-language 14-item document list) sits in both the MBSJ and MP Sepang folders, so the two councils are processed off a common base set with council-specific overlays.
